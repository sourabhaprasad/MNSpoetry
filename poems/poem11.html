Wanderer

Where have you been wondering lately ? Are you in deepest forest finding a cure ? Or are you in the wild, surviving on those diseased thoughts ? Are you in that beautiful valley you created ? Or are you in that destroyed barren lands? Are you on those high mountains hearing your echoes ? Or are you being carried by the waved of your past ? Are you a lost soul finding his way back home ? Or are you a soul lost in his own home ?
