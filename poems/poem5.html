Into the Darkness

Into the limitless path of dying hope, I weapon the withering light. A maze looks like an undiscovered path they said, A nomad's perfect muse they didn't warn of. And thus, into the maze I ponder. Deeper the roads, familiar the neighbouring, The faces I know, the stories I don't. Resonating the paths but played my our own. Some resisting the fate some accepting it as their own. A home, we wish had never found our paths, An aimless journey to the comfort of discovering the unknown. We depart to a maze we call Pain.
