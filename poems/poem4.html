Clouds

We are like the clouds. On our way to an unknown destination too light to stay, Too heavy to hold on to. Too scattered, too dense. Carving our own shapes, We crush and collide, the shade and the bright. Creating an illusion of an end of the heights, Covering the mystery above the heights. We consume from what destroys us later. A boon or a Bane Or a droplet that dreamt of the heights.
