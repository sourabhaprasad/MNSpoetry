The Night

How enduring is the night Silence engulfing itself The heavy breathes soothing the air around us as we drift. A pause of reality . And yet , The mind wanders to a place looking for one, where I am more closer to be felt A place thats forgotten when I wake And yet I wake up to be forgotten in someone's dream. I wake up to a reality of silence within striving through out the day Until the silence engulfs the surrounding again.
