The Sky Above Me

The sky above me is different than yours, Two wanderers searching for the same home. And when the clouds hit, For a glimpse atleast, the world felt like a factual illusion. The walks on the streets, hearing the talks of the trees. Pouring is the rain, washing all our words in vain. We walk the same steps , in the same reps. A home for two, the magic felt by very few. The clock strikes 12, And that's how two old souls met to dwell.
