Your Light

Your light passing through me, To our future far apart to be. You are the star far away to reach And I'm the immobile planet restrained to leave. I follow your spins watching your rythms. A never setting star, leaving me with a scar. Someday We aligned with our future Nearing you and the future, concealing your brightness They call it an eclipse Perfectly aligned, starts to decline A beautiful disaster lasting for few.
